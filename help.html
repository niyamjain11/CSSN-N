How do I navigate the website?

expand_more

Use the main menu at the top of the page. You'll find links to sections like 'Home', 'Learn', 'Play', and 'Parents & Teachers'. Each section contains various resources and activities. You can also use the search bar to find specific topics.

Where can I find learning resources?

expand_more

Our learning resources are located under the 'Learn' tab in the main menu. They are categorized by topic and age group to help you find exactly what you need for your little explorer.

How do I access fun activities?

expand_more

Head over to the 'Play' section! There you will find a variety of interactive games, quizzes, and downloadable activity sheets designed to make learning about the environment fun and engaging.
