Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Using the Website

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner and fill in your details. You’ll receive a confirmation email to activate your account.

How do I navigate the site?

Use the top menu to explore sections like "Start Learning," "Eco Fun Zone," and "Teacher & Parent Area." You can also use the search bar to find specific topics.

What if I forget my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it via your registered email.

Learning Resources

Where can I find study materials?

Visit the "Start Learning" page for curated lessons, videos, and downloadable worksheets on environmental topics.

Are the materials suitable for all ages?

Yes! We provide resources tailored for kids, young learners, and even adults interested in environmental studies.

Fun Activities

How do I access games and quizzes?

Head over to the "Eco Fun Zone" where you’ll find interactive games, quizzes, and eco-challenges designed to make learning fun!

Can I participate in eco-challenges?

Absolutely! Join our monthly eco-challenges and share your progress with the community.

Parental & Teacher Guides

Where can parents and teachers find guides?

Visit the "Teacher & Parent Area" for lesson plans, tips, and resources to support young learners.

How can I get involved as a teacher?

Contact us via the form below or email to learn about partnership opportunities and resource contributions.
